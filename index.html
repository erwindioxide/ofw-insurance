For only USD 70, you are entitled the following packages and benefits:

Natural death, with at least $10,000 survivor’s benefit.

Accidental death, with at least $15,000 survivor’s benefit.

Repatriation Benefit - transport of OFW back to the Philippines including personal belongings.
