How to Apply

Apply in just three easy steps!


Eligilibity:

Who are eligible to apply?

● For clients aged 21 to 55 years old.

● Must be in good health.

● Must be an OFW with a valid employment contract and with a valid working visa/permit.



Steps in applying:

Step 1

Download the necessary documents

Download, print, fill out, and sign the Rampver Financials Data Privacy Consent Form and the OFW insurance application form.


Step 2

Pay for your insurance premium

Pay for the insurance premium through your preferred payment center or portal, either by Local or Overseas payment methods.


Step 3

Prepare and Submit your Documents

Prepare the following documents (click on each item to view sample):

  • Signed Rampver Financials Data Privacy Consent (DPC) Form
  • Signed OFW Insurance Application Form
  • Valid Passport (with photo and signature)
  • Working Visa or Work Permit
  • Proof of Payment (Clear Scanned Copy or Screenshot)

Once all the documents and requirements have been prepared, kindly submit it for checking and verification.

Upload the clear copy your documents with our Online Application Facility OR email it to ofwinsurance@rampver.com.

You will receive a confirmation email once approved, and you will get your Certificate of Insurance through your nominated email address within 10 banking days.
