How to Claim


Step 1

Check if you have the required documents. Here is the list of all documents needed for specific claim benefit. Kindly make sure to prepare all the needed documents to prevent any delay or confusion when making a claim.


Step 2

Send us an email with ALL the required documents attached through OFinsurance@rampver.com.


Step 3

Please wait for our OFW claims department to contact you within 10 days upon submission of complete documents. For any other inquiries or concerns, kindly send us an email at OFinsurance@rampver.com.



List of required documents


  • Authenticated Death Certificate issued by Philippines foreign post
  • Death Certificate from the local Civil Registrar
  • Incident Report from employer
  • Police Report in case of accident
  • Employment Contract and OEC (Overseas Employment Certificate)
  • Original POC and Receipt

  • Medical Certificate
  • Accident Report from employer or police
  • Picture of the disabled person or affected parts of the body
  • Employment Contract and OEC (Overseas Employment Certificate)/li>

  • Marriage Contract of insured and spouse, if beneficiary is the spouse
  • Affidavit of Legal Guardianship, if beneficiary is a minor

  • POLO (Philippine Overseas Labor Office) Certificate which states the reason/s for termination of the OF’s employment and the need for his/her repatriation by the Philippines foreign post
  • Travel Itinerary with Invoice
  • Official Receipt/ Proof of payment
  • Employment Contract & OEC (Overseas Employment Certificate)
  • Passport/Visa with arrival & departure stamp
  • OFW Affidavit
