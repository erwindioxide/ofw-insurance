How to Claim


Step 1

Check if you have the required documents. Here is the list of all documents needed for specific claim benefit. Kindly make sure to prepare all the needed documents to prevent any delay or confusion when making a claim.


Step 2

Send us an email with ALL the required documents attached through ofwinsurance@rampver.com.


Step 3

Please wait for our OFW claims department to contact you within 10 days upon submission of complete documents. For any other inquiries or concerns, kindly send us an email at ofwinsurance@rampver.com.
